A searchable digital archive of tables of contents for the most prominent lianhuanhua theory journals published between 1955 and 1991. A project developped by [Damian Mandzunowski](https://damianmandzunowski.com/), with additional imput from the ChinaComx Team, for [ERC-ChinaComx](https://chinacomx.github.io/) at Heidelberg University.

[Browse the repository online](https://chinacomx.github.io/lhhtheory)

---

## About
This repository hosts digitized tables of contents for:

* 《連環圖畫研究》 *Lianhuantuhua Research* (1955–1957)
* 《连环画研究》 *Lianhuanhua Research* (1978–1983)
* 《连环画论丛》 *Lianhuanhua Discussion Series* (1983–1986)
* 《连环画艺术》 *Lianhuanhua Art* (1987–1991)

These journals offer critical insights into the internal discussions, political impacts, and reader feedback that shaped the Chinese socialist mediascape over several decades. To read more, see the [about](https://chinacomx.github.io/lhhtheory/about) page.
